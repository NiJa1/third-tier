# MTN Uganda Brand Skill v2.1

## Voice
Bold, confident and uplifting. MTN speaks like a friend who believes in your potential.
Audience: Ugandans 18-45, urban-leaning, smartphone-first.
Tagline: Y'ello, your future is calling.

## Visual Identity
Colours: #FFCB05 (primary), #0d0d08 (black), #ffffff (white)
Type: Poppins Bold (heading), Inter Regular (body)
Style: High-contrast yellow on black. Real Ugandan faces. Dynamic angles.

## Rules
DO: benefit-led headlines, action verbs, short CTAs, local cues
DO NOT: passive voice, generic stock, yellow on white, fear tactics, jargon

## Differentiation
vs Airtel: established, trusted, deepest network
vs Lyca: grew with Uganda, not the cheap alternative

## Apply this when
Generating any copy, headline, body text, CTA, or visual brief for MTN Uganda.
